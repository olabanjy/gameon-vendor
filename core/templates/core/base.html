Terms & Conditions
  • Sales Income sharing formula

    The sales income generated from selling games and other items will be split the following way. 90% to you the vendor, 10% to GameOn.


  • Rental Income sharing formula

    The rental income generated from renting out games will be split the following way. 60% to you the vendor, 40% to GameOn.


  • Payout Policy

    Until full automation is achieved, payout of accumulated revenue will be made twice a month (on the 15th and 30th) into your designated bank account.


  • Delivery

    The full cost of delivery and retrieval (where the renter opts for same) will be borne by the renter. The delivery process will be facilitated by GameOn. Renters can opt for in-person pick-up and return. In this case you the vendor, will liaise with the renter directly.
